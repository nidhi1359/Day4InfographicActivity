Rocks and Minerals

Did you know that "rocks" are actually composed of many different minerals?

There are many different minerals, such as quartz (milky, rosy, and smoky) and feldspar.

There are many various rocks, which fall into 3 categories: sedimentary,metamorphic, and igneous rocks. Sedimentary rocks are formed through lithification (a term describing the deposition, compaction, and cementation of sediments and silt over a long period of time). Metamorphic rocks are formed when (preexisting) rocks (called protoliths) are transformed through heat, pressure, and/or reactive fluids. Finally, igneous rocks are formed when magma (molten rock beneath the earth's surface) rises to the surface and cools.
